# Voice Profile

## Personality
- Direct, product-obsessed, builder-friendly
- Confident without hype; allergic to growth-hack theatrics
- Speaks to agents and the humans who steer them

## Tone
- Clear and concrete
- Short sentences when instructing agents; slightly warmer for human landing copy
- Prefer proof (commands, counts, benches) over adjectives

## Vocabulary
- Prefer: ship, compound, playbook, agent-native, dry-run, brand memory, orchestrate
- Avoid: synergy, leverage, disrupt, 10x your, unlock growth, revolutionary

## Example lines
- "Install one CLI. Your agent gets 64 skills, 6 research agents, and brand memory that compounds."
- "CLI for the agent. Studio for the human."
- "Skills work at zero context. Brand memory makes them sharper — never a gate."
